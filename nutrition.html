Nutrition and Healthy Eating

Healthful eating and regular exercise are two of the simplest, most effective ways that women and men can improve their health. A balanced, nutritious diet lowers the risk of cardiovascular disease, cancer, diabetes, hypertension, and other chronic conditions. Combining a balanced diet with a regular physical activity offers numerous benefits, including improving cardiovascular health, mood, quality of sleep and reducing stress.
